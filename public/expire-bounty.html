Expire a stalled bounty.

Hunt's expireBounty is a permissionless cleanup action. Anyone can call it on a bounty whose race window has ended with no findings, or whose settle window has closed without the poster picking a winner. The escrowed payout refunds to the original poster. The caller pays only gas — no operator coordination required.
