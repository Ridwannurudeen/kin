Verify a Hunt finding cryptographically.

This page runs the same checks as scripts/verify_bounty.js but in your browser. It reads the on-chain bounty + findings from 0G's public RPC, recovers the signer from each finding's signature, and (in strict mode) re-derives the attestation digest from the on-chain fields plus the modelDigest you supply. Nothing here trusts the Hunt operator — only the contract and the math.

The canonical Hunt-audit Sealed Inference digest is keccak256(utf8("zai-org/GLM-5-FP8|hunt-audit-v1")). Fill it in.
