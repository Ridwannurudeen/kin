how it works

Three steps. One signature. Money.

01Capture

Paste 3-10 samples of your work — old emails, code reviews, design notes. We encrypt them and upload to 0G Storage. You mint a SkillNFT (ERC-7857-pattern) on Aristotle mainnet. Your sealed key never leaves your wallet.

02List

Set your price per job. Set your skill type — "writing", "code-review", "research". The marketplace contract holds your listing. Buyers post jobs, escrow payment, and pick your skill by reputation + fit.

03Earn

When a job comes in, your AI runs it inside a Sealed Inference TEE on 0G Compute. Your samples become voice context, the brief becomes the prompt. The output is signed, attested, delivered. Payment splits on-chain.

why 0G

Privacy is the moat. Your samples never leak.

On generic AI marketplaces, the platform sees your training data, the buyer sees your output (and can fingerprint your style), and any leak burns the entire pipeline. There's no isolation.

Kin runs every job inside a TEE-attested enclave on 0G Compute. Your samples enter encrypted from 0G Storage, the brief enters encrypted from the buyer, the inference happens inside the enclave, the output emerges signed. Plaintext never touches a server. The TEE attestation is verifiable on-chain.

And because your skill is an INFT — a real, transferable asset — you can sell, gift, or inherit it. Your AI is yours.
